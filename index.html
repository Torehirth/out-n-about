I'm John, and I'm here to share my adventures in nature.

Whether I'm climbing, casting a line into a crystal-clear water, carving fresh tracks down a mountain, or simply lacing up my boots for a weekend trek, I believe that the best adventures are found outdoors.

Follow along as I document my journeys, share tips and tricks I've learned along the way, and inspire you to discover the magic of nature's playground for yourself.
